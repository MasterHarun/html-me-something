Bosnia

The birthplace of something great

Somewhere deep in the beautiful landscape of a small little country called Bosnia a thought was brought upon the universe. This thought wasn't much during the time but eventually grew to become something bigger than even the originator could of imagined.

Bosnia is a small country with an expanse of history stemming through the dark ages. Its people grew and cultivated a land and culture that has stood the test of time.

World

The world is in your palm

The world is always ready to experience what happens next. The creator bestows upon the world that which he wills and the participants have no choice but to follow. Wether the inhabitants believe it or not the thing we call an experience was already set forth in motion long before it was thought of in the mind.

To think we have ultimate control of our life is to be blinded by the mind. A single point of will has the power to change the course of the world and how you experience it.
